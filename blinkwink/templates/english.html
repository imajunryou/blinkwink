Below are links to my final paper for my Introduction to Writing class. We were tasked with deciding upon a topic of our choosing, one relevant to the world today, and to research some aspect of it. In particular, we were to pose a question, explore its facets, and then provide some semblance of an answer.

I have chosen to explore a topic of personal interest. The "alignment problem" as found in artificial intelligence research. This problem is concerned with ensuring that the effective values of a general artificial intelligence remain consistent and aligned with the common values of humanity, across modifications and over time.

The annotated bibliography consists of a subset of my sources, with some short summarization of their contents and a few words about my intentions for their future use in my paper. The paper itself follows in the next link below that, with a complete (unannotated) bibliography at the end.
